# Oatmeal Molasses Bread
---
Yield: 2 loaves

## Ingredients
1 1/2 cup boiling water
1 cup rolled oats
3/4 cup molasses
3 tbsp soft butter or vegetable oil
2 tsp sea salt
1 tbsp dry yeast dissolved in ...
2 cups lukewarm water (let sit for 8-10 min)
8 cups unbleached flour



## Instructions
1. Pour boiling water over oats and let stand for 30 min.
2. Then add molasses, oil, salt, yeast mixture
3. Mix, then slowly add flour.
