Cream cheese dip snacks:

Salmony Luxury:
1 1/2 dl Philadelphia cheese
100 g hot smoked salmon
3 tbsp chopped dill
1 tsp squeezed lemon juice
1 ripe avocado

Proceed as follows: Blend cheese, salmon and dill. Season with a little salt, black pepper and lemon. Decorate with dill and avocado.

Josefina's Finest:
1 1/2 cream cheese
1/2 dl grilled peppers (available in cans),
finely chopped 1/2 grated garlic clove
1 pcs fresh red pepper

Proceed as follows: Mix all the ingredients. Season with a little salt and black pepper. Decorate with fresh peppers.

Apple and Horseradish Mix:
1 1/2 dl cream cheese
1 grated apple
3 tbsp grated horseradish
3 tbsp chopped dill 
2 tsp squeezed lemon juice

Proceed as follows: Mix all the ingredients. Season with a little salt and black pepper. Decorate with dill and apple.






Simple old man mess
1 1/2 dl cream cheese
4 tbsp chopped anchovies
2 tbsp chopped red onion
3 tbsp chopped chives
3 tbsp chopped dill
1 tsp squeezed lemon juice

Proceed as follows: Mix all the ingredients. Season with a little salt and black pepper. Decorate with dill and chives.

Boathouse mess:
1 dl cream cheese
1/2 spreadable liver pâté
2 tbsp roasted onions
1 tbsp chopped chives
8 slices pickles, finely chopped

Proceed as follows: Mix all the ingredients. Decorate with chives, finely chopped pickles, roasted onions and a little black pepper.

Dill and Shrimp Mix:
1 dl cream cheese
1 dl chopped shrimp
3 tbsp chopped chives
4 tbsp chopped dill
1 tsp squeezed lemon juice

Proceed as follows: Mix all the ingredients. Season with a little salt and black pepper. Decorate with shrimp and dill.
