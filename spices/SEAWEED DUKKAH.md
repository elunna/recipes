# SEAWEED DUKKAH
---
Yield: 1 1/2 cups

## Ingredients
1 cup macadamias
2 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp coriander seeds
2 tbsp white sesame seeds
1/4 cup dulse flakes or 4 nori sheets
2 tsp dried Greek oregano
sea salt and freshy ground black pepper

Toast the macadamias in a skillet over a medium heat until golden (~4 min). Remove from the pan and leave to cool, then finely chop.

Grind the seeds (and nori if using) finely in a blender (or use a mortar and pestle). Add to the pan and toast until fragrant (~2 min). Cool, then stir in the macadamias, oregano, and dulse flakes. Will keep for 1 month in an airtight container.
