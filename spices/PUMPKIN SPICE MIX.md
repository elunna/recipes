# PUMPKIN SPICE MIX
---
Yield:  1/2 cup

## Ingredients
1/3 cup ground cinnamon
1 tbsp ground ginger
1 tbsp ground nutmeg
3 tsp ground allspice


Put all the ingredients in an airtight jar, seal with the lid and shake. Keeps for 6 months.
