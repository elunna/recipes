## Ingredients
1 cup almond milk
2 to 3 handfuls of leafy greens
1 1/2 cup frozen mango
1 1/2 cup frozen peach slices
1 tbsp hemp seeds
1 tbsp buckwheat groats
1 tbsp maca powder, optional
1 tsp matcha powder

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients together.
