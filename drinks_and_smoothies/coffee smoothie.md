# Coffee Smoothie
---
Yield: 1 serving (16-oz)

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 cup cold brew or cold strong coffee
- 1 ripe banana, cut into chunks and frozen
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 large pitted Medjool date
- 1 tsp cocoa powder
- 2 ice cubes

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients together.

## Notes
- 429	Calories
- 16g	Fat
- 66g	Carbs
- 12g	Protein
