## Ingredients
1 cup green tea chilled
1/2 cup plain non fat Greek yogurt
1 cup raspberries fresh or frozen
1 large frozen banana cut into chunks
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup ice

## Instructions
1. Blend all ingredients together.
